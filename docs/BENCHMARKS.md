# Benchmarks

Machine-readable measured results are under `data/benchmarks/`.

- Executor: startup, completion, tool-call validity, memory, decode rate.
- Planner/reviewer: structured role output, latency, memory, decode rate.
- Resident: two-model and final three-model memory headroom.
- Gateway: health overhead, end-to-end tool-call latency, free disk.

No unmeasured value is reported as a benchmark.
